Our Story

From fixing devices to building skilled technicians.


iFix India started in 2009 as a specialized repair service focused on solving problems others couldn't - especially at the motherboard level. Over the years, working on thousands of iPhones and MacBooks gave us deep, practical expertise in advanced repairs.

But one challenge kept repeating - a shortage of technicians who could handle complex repairs confidently. That gap led to the creation of iFix India Training Academy, where we now train students using real devices, real faults, and real repair processes used in professional labs.
