Our Story

From repair benches to a national academy.

Founded in 2009, iFix India began with a mission to make high-quality repair affordable and reliable. As our service centers grew, we saw the industry shortage: skilled technicians.

iFix Academy was created to close that gap. We train students with the same tools, procedures, and standards used in professional repair environments. The result is graduates who are ready for real work.
